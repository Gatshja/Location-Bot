Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2023

This Privacy Policy explains how Location Bot collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Discord bot and website services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Location Bot, we may collect the following information:

Data Type Purpose Storage Duration
Discord User ID Command tracking, user analytics 30 days
Discord Username Command log attribution 30 days
Discord Server ID Service operation, analytics 30 days
Discord Server Name Service operation, analytics 30 days
Command Usage Service improvement, bug tracking 30 days
Location Queries Processing your requests Temporary (not stored)

We do not collect or store personal information beyond what is necessary to provide our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To improve user experience and bot functionality
  • To track command usage for optimization
  • To analyze usage patterns and trends
  • To detect and address technical issues or abuse

Usage information is analyzed in aggregate and is not used to identify individual users beyond their Discord identifiers.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We use the following third-party services to provide our functionality:

  • LocationIQ - For geocoding and map services
  • Yandex Maps - Alternative map provider
  • Open Notify API - For International Space Station tracking

When you use location-related commands, your queries (such as place names or coordinates) are forwarded to these services to provide results. We do not share your Discord user information with these services.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Command logs are stored in secured server environments and are automatically deleted after 30 days.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request information about what data we have about you
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your data from our logs
  • Opt-out: You can stop using our services at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information in the "Contact" section.

6. Data Retention

We retain usage logs for a maximum of 30 days. After this period, logs are automatically deleted from our systems.

Aggregated statistics may be retained for longer periods but do not contain personally identifiable information.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 13 years of age, in compliance with Discord's Terms of Service. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
